#####Front#####

1. TO THE TRVE TOVCH
OF WITTE, IN THE SPIRIT
of the best vnderstanding in a Gentlewoman
(worthie of much honour) Mistris
Anne Breton, of Little Catthorpe
in Leicestershire, Nicho. Breton
wisheth all eternall
happines.

1. TO THE READER.

#####Body#####

1. OLD MAD-CAPS
new Gally-mawfrey.

    _ 1. Mad-caps I will not.

    _ 2. Mad-caps Oh the
merrie time.

    _ 3
MAD-CAPS
Out vpon Money.
